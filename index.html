Who We Are

Who We Are

At CRU Augusta, we believe college is one of the most pivotal seasons of life. We exist to help students discover who they are, why they're here, and how they can change the world for Christ.

Our mission is simple: Win — Reach the Campus. Build — Grow in Faith. Send — Impact the World.

Our goal is to help students not only grow in their relationship with God, but also learn how to share their faith and make an impact on campus.
