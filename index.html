Who We Are

College is one of the most pivotal seasons of your life.

We're a movement of students at Augusta University helping people know Jesus Christ, grow in their faith, and discover a life that truly matters.

As part of Cru, we're passionate about reaching our campus, building authentic community, and sending students out to change the world.

Win — reach the campus.
Build — grow in faith.
Send — impact the world.
