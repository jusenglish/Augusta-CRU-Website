Who We Are

About Cru at Augusta

Bill and Vonette Bright launched Campus Crusade for Christ in 1951 to "win the campus today and change the world tomorrow." Today, Cru is a movement on more than 1,000 campuses around the world.

At Augusta University, we exist to help students know God, find community, and be sent out to make a difference — on campus and beyond.
