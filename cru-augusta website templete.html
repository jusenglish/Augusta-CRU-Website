Who We Are

More than a club.
A community.

At CRU Augusta, we believe college is one of the most pivotal seasons of life. We exist to help students discover who they are, why they're here, and how they can change the world for Christ.

1

Win — Reach the Campus

We actively share the love and message of Jesus Christ with every student at Augusta University.

2

Build — Grow in Faith

Through Bible study, discipleship, and genuine relationships, we help students go deeper with God.

3

Send — Impact the World

We equip graduates to carry the gospel into careers, families, and nations after they leave AU.

What We Believe

Rooted in historic Christian faith.

We hold to the Scriptures as our authority, Jesus Christ as our center, and the Great Commission as our call — on every campus, in every nation.


Full Statement of Faith →
📜

Scripture

The Bible is the inspired, authoritative Word of God — our guide for faith, life, and purpose.

✝️

Jesus Christ

Fully God and fully man — He died, rose again, and is the only path to restored relationship with God.

🕊️

The Holy Spirit

The Spirit indwells every believer, empowering us to live godly lives and share the gospel boldly.

🌐

Great Commission

Every believer is called to make disciples — starting here on campus, reaching every nation.

"Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit."
Matthew 28:19
