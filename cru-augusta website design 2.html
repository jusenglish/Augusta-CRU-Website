About Cru

Who We Are

Bill and Vonette Bright launched Campus Crusade for Christ as a campus ministry in 1951 to "win the campus today and change the world tomorrow." Today, Cru is a network of movements on more than 1,000 college campuses around the world.

At Augusta University, we exist to build a community where students can grow in faith, connect with others, and be sent out to make a difference.
